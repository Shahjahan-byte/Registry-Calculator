Registry Process Guide

What is FBR Tax Filer status? The Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) maintains an Active Taxpayer List (ATL). If you file your income tax return, you are a "Filer" and pay lower withholding tax rates. Non-filers pay significantly higher rates — up to 10.5% for some charges.

Step-by-Step Process
Step 1 — Verify FBR Status
Both buyer and seller should check their ATL status on the FBR website before proceeding. This determines your applicable rates.
Step 2 — Agree on Property Value
The DC (Deputy Commissioner) rate and agreed market value both matter. Registry fees are charged on the higher of DC value or declared value.
Step 3 — Pay Challan
Fees are deposited via bank challan (PSID) at any branch of designated banks. Obtain receipt before registry appointment.
Step 4 — Registry at PLRA Arzi Centre
Visit your local PLRA Arzi Centre or Tehsil office with CNIC, challans, title documents, and both parties present.
